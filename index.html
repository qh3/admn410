History

the Durham House of Pets (DHP) was the founded by Mr.&Mrs. TT after they retired in 2007. DHP started as a homeless dog shelter quickly became a business. Over the years, Durham House of Pets has expanded their products to include dog supplies, cat supplies, and snake supplies.

Why use us?

We focus on helping homeless pets to find new home! We are Reliable and Professional, and also, we have helped thousands of families to find their lovely dogs, cats, or snakes in the past decade. We believe we can help you choose your new friend and offer quality services to you.
